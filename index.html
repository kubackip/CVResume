Projects

Engineering Thesis
Title of my engineer thesis was "Alternative-Augmentative Communication software for children with cerebral palsy". I've made Java desktop application, where the user could communicate by means of sentences composed of pictograms of his choice. Application were sending a text message to the Android app on smartphone, using UDP.
Engineering-Thesis.git
Let's do a settlement
Have you ever been at a party, and different people shopped for different amounts of money, and you couldn't figure out who should give money back to whom? Have you ever shopped but wanted to deduct something you bought just for yourself from your overall bill? Using this desktop application you can make a settlement up to 10 people.
LetsDoSettlement.git
